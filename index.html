What is Dream Convention

Where
Dreams
Get Built

Dream Convention is a one-night pitch competition that brings together the brightest entrepreneurial minds from the University of St. Thomas and the University of Minnesota.

Hear directly from four CEOs who built something from nothing. Their stories, their failures, their breakthroughs. Then take the stage and pitch your own.

Real judges. Real prize money. A real shot at turning your idea into something more.
